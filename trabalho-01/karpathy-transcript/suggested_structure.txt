Suggested Structure (flexible, not rigid)
(Lecture2_PPgEEC.pdf — page 14)

You are encouraged, but not forced, to organize your videos around:

1. The Big Picture
   Explain the overall pipeline:
   - Tokens → Embeddings → Transformer → Output
   - What the model is actually doing
   - Focus on clarity and conceptual flow.

2. Technical Deep Dive
   Explain core technical mechanisms in more detail:
   - Attention mechanism (intuition + formal perspective)
   - Transformer blocks (self-attention, feed-forward layers)
   - Training process (objective, data, scaling)
   - Inference behavior
   - Students are encouraged to include diagrams, equations, or
     examples where appropriate.

3. Critical Reflection
   Provide a critical and analytical perspective:
   - What are the limitations of LLMs?
   - What aspects were unclear or under-explained in the source material?
   - What are common misconceptions?
   - What questions remain open?
   - This section should demonstrate independent thinking,
     not just reproduction of content.
